## Overview

Axio Marketing operates in a competitive but fragmented local market. The Staffordshire Moorlands sub-region (Leek, Biddulph, Cheadle, Uttoxeter, the Peak District fringe) is significantly under-served by local agencies compared to Stafford, Stoke-on-Trent, and Cannock. This creates an opportunity to dominate hyper-local terms at lower competition while using broader county terms to build volume. Axio's USP — build first, pay after approval — is a differentiator with its own keyword angle ("no upfront cost website", "see your website before you pay").

Competition levels below are assessed relative to the Staffordshire local market (not national UK). "Low" means few or no well-optimised local pages for that term. "High" means multiple DA 30+ agencies have dedicated, optimised pages for it.
